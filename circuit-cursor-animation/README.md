**Project Title: Circuit Cursor Animation**

**Project Overview:**
This is an interactive web-based visualizer that generates dynamic, circuit-inspired line patterns trailing behind the user's cursor. The animation mimics the aesthetic of digital currents flowing through a motherboard, featuring sharp 45-degree turns and neon-style glows.

**Key Features:**

* **Interactive Trail:** Real-time particle generation that follows mouse movements or touch inputs.
* **Circuit Logic:** Particles move in straight lines and snap to 45-degree or 90-degree angles to maintain a technical "PCB" look.
* **Theme Switching:** A toggle button with animated Sun and Moon icons to switch between high-contrast Dark Mode and a clean Light Mode.
* **Responsive Design:** Fully fluid canvas that adapts to any screen size and supports mobile touch interactions.
* **Smooth Visuals:** Uses high-performance requestAnimationFrame for fluid motion and semi-transparent clearing for a "ghosting" trail effect.
